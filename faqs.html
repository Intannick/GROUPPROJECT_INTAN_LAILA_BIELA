FAQS-Payment

List of payment option 
We accept VISA & MASTERCARD credit/debit cards (ipay88/Paypal), GrabPay, and more. The list of payment method options will be displayed upon checking out.

Cash on delivery

We do not facilitate Cash On Delivery (COD) service at the moment.

Grab pay later is a payment method where you can choose to pay via installment with 0% interest in 4 monthly installment. Simply choose to checkout with Grab, key in the OTP send to your mobile and select Paylater as your payment method. You can visit GRAB website to more details. 

 

FAQS-Shipping

How long it will takes for delivery process ? 
Peninsular Malaysia it will take 3 - 5 working days.
Sabah & Sarawak it will take 5 - 10 working days.

(Note: The delivery might been delayed due to few factors such as bad weather or arrangement from the courier provider itself)

How long will it ready to be pickup?

The ordered item(s) placed on weekdays (Monday-Friday) will be ready for self-pickup after ONE working day. If you place an order during weekends and public holidays, please expect that the order will be ready to collect in the next TWO working days. However, this is subject to our email notification stating that your order is ready to be picked up during the boutique's operating hours.

How long do I have to pickup my order?

You are required to collect your ordered items within 7 days after receiving an order confirmation email. Failure to collect within the period given, your ordered item(s) will be returned to our warehouse.

Item found defective/damaged during self-collect

You shall inspect your ordered item(s) immediately upon collection/receiving for any deficiencies, defective/damaged products, and wrong item/physical product such as colour and size; as we are not liable for it after you exit the boutique.
